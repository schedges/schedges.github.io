  • May 29, 2024

    nEXO supernova paper submitted to arXiv

    A nEXO paper studying supernova electron-neutrino charged-current interactions with xenon was recently submitted to arXiv. This paper models electron-neutrino charged-current interactions with xenon at supernova neutrino energies. Although nEXO's total mass will be only ~5-tonnes, it will have the potential to observe supernova neutrinos from hundreds of nearby candidate stars.

  • November 29, 2023

    Publication on the first inclusive neutrino-127I charged-current measurement

    COHERENT's paper on the first inclusive electron-neutrino charged-current measurement on 127I was just published! We found a cross section lower than predicted, with the likely origin a suppression in the neutron-emitting exclusive cross section. This agrees with results from COHERENT's recent lead neutrino cube measurement!

    I was part of a recent workshop on developing kiloTonne Xenon detectors for neutrinoless double beta decay. Going to the kTonne-scale is going to be challenging, but there is a lot of interesting physics to search for in a detector of this size.

  • October 3, 2023

    Measurement of Neutrino-Induced Neutrons Published!

    COHERENT's measurement of neutrino-induced neutrons on lead was recently published! This detector observed a significant reduction in the neutron-emitting exclusive cross section compared to theoretical predictions. Combined with a preliminary measurement, we find the cross section is lower by a factor of 0.29.
